How It Works

Wand properties

A witch or wizard channelled their magical powers to centralise the effects for more complex results.

Although they could not think or communicate like a human, they could perform certain actions through their own will. This ability may have been how they chose their wizard or witch and also explains Harry's wand autonomously acting against Voldemort. They could perform actions such as picking their owners, changing allegiances, and, in certain circumstances, perform magic on their own.

Pros. Albus Percival Wulfric Brian Dumbledore

The greatest wizard alive

Here's a time line of Pros. Dumbledore's life:

  • 1881 - Born to Percival and Kendra Dumbledore.
  • 1892 - Attends Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and is sorted into Gryffindor house.
  • 1890 - After graduating, Dumbledore's family is struck by tragedy when his sister Ariana dies during a three-way duel between Albus, his brother Aberforth, and Gellert Grindelwald.
  • 1900s-1940s - Builds a distinguished career as a scholar and professor of Transfiguration at Hogwarts.
  • 1945 - Defeats the dark wizard Gellert Grindelwald in a legendary duel, earning widespread fame.
  • 1956 - Appointed Headmaster of Hogwarts.
  • 1970s - Forms and leads the Order of the Phoenix to fight against Voldemort and his Death Eaters during the First Wizarding War.
  • 1991 - Meets Harry Potter and plays a pivotal role in guiding him in his fight against Voldemort.
  • 1996 - Discovers and begins to destroy Voldemort’s Horcruxes.
  • 1997 - Dies on the Astronomy Tower, killed by Severus Snape as part of a plan to ultimately defeat Voldemort.
  • 1998 - Continues to influence events even after his death, particularly through his will and memories, aiding Harry in his final battle against Voldemort.

“Help will always be given at Hogwarts to those who deserve it.”
